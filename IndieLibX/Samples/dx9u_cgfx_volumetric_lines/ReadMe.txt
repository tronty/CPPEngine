Direct3D version of the CG Volume Lines sample from the NVidia SDK.


Original OpenGL sample:
http://http.download.nvidia.com/developer/SDK/Individual_Samples/DEMOS/OpenGL/VolumeLine.zip

White paper:
http://http.download.nvidia.com/developer/SDK/Individual_Samples/DEMOS/OpenGL/src/cg_VolumeLine/docs/VolumeLine.pdf

Controls:
Click and drag to spin the bunch of lines around.
Arrow keys to move the bunch of lines around
+ and - control line thickness.
Page down (PgDn) to cycle through the available textures.
